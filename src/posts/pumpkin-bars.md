### Ingredients

- 4 eggs
- 1 2/3 cup sugar
- 1 cup cooking oil
- 1 16 oz. can pumpkin
- 2 cups flour
- 2 tsp. baking powder
- 2 tsp. cinnamon
- 1 tsp. salt
- 1 tsp. baking soda

### Directions

Mix all ingredients together until well blended. Spread mixture on well-greased jelly roll pan, or cookie sheet with sides. 
Bake about 25-30 min. @ 350. Cool. Spread cream cheese frosting on top.
